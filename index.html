Hi!
We are Speaxie, a young and passionate team of professional English teachers!
We both lived and worked in China for more than 6 years. Since we gained most of our teaching experience in China, we know a lot about peculiarities of working with Chinese kids of all ages.
We utilized our experience to develop an outstanding program with a unique approach to every student to help them master their English skills through fun and exciting interactive experience.
We promise that you'll never feel bored during our classes and we guarantee that you'll notice improvements in your speaking and listening just after few short classes.
Looking forward to bringing your English to a whole new level!
